## 🔍 Scanning for malware

Security risks hide in many spots. BeagleX searches your files to find these threats. 

1. Open the application.
2. Select the Scan button on the main dashboard.
3. Wait for the progress bar to finish.
4. Review the results list.

If the app finds a threat, it provides options to quarantine or delete the file. Do not worry if the scan takes a few minutes. It checks every folder for your safety.

## 🧹 Managing computer memory

Computer speed often decreases when background apps use too much power. Our memory tool stops these processes. 

Open the dashboard and look for the Memory usage graph. If the bar is red, the computer needs help. Click the Clean button to free up space. This action closes idle apps that consume your resources. Your computer will feel faster right after this task.

## 📊 Monitoring system health

Know exactly what happens inside your system. The Monitoring tab shows live data about your hardware. 

*   **CPU Usage:** See how much work your processor does right now.
*   **Disk Activity:** Watch how files move across your storage.
*   **Battery Health:** Check the life of your battery to decide if it needs a charge.

Open this tab once a day to make sure your machine runs smoothly. High CPU usage often points to apps that run in the background without your consent. You can identify these apps and close them from the list provided.

## ❓ Frequently asked questions

**Does the app slow down my computer during a scan?** 
No. We designed the scanner to use only a small amount of system resources. You can still use other apps while it runs.

**Can I stop a scan before it finishes?** 
Yes. Click the Cancel button at any time. The app will save the progress made until that moment.

**Is this safe to use?** 
Yes. BeagleX respects your privacy. It never sends your personal files to our servers. All scanning happens on your own hardware.

**What do I do if I find a false positive?** 
Sometimes an app gets marked as a threat wrongly. You can add those files to a whitelist in the Settings menu.
